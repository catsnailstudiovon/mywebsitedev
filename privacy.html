Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Investment Calculator mobile application published by Cats Nail Studio Dev.

1. Information We Collect

Investment Calculator is designed as a financial planning and calculation tool. User-entered values such as investment amounts, contribution amounts, return rates, and saved plans may be stored locally on the user’s device to support app functionality.

2. Personal Data

We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information through the app unless the user chooses to contact us directly by email.

3. Local Storage

Some calculation data and saved plans may be stored locally on the device so the user can reopen scenarios later. This data is not sold or shared by us.

4. PDF Export

When a user exports results to PDF, the generated file is created for the user’s convenience. The user controls how and where that file is shared.

5. Third-Party Services

If third-party services are added in future app versions, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

6. Financial Disclaimer

Investment Calculator is provided for educational and planning purposes only. It does not provide financial services, lending, payment processing, trading, brokerage, or professional financial advice.
